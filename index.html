Other kick-ass hacks

The ones that didn't make the cut that we think are really cool.

Bloomout

Bloomout allows management and HR teams in companies prevent employee burnout and helps reduce employee attrition. This project was made during Swiss Re Hackathon 2017 in Bratislava.

Lorien

Lorien is a profitable and sustainable forest asset management platform. It connects forest owners with consumers and allows them to invest in the forest aiding forest conservation. This hack was made during Ultrahack 2017 in Helsinki.
